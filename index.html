• Dream Machine

    Hallucinate Creative

    Dream Machine turns your smartphone into a psychedelic drug. The app is based on an old method of creating visual hallucinations through flickering light at a specific speed. This technique has been used by William S. Burroughs, Iggy Pop, and most notably Nostradamus. It is thought that Nostradamus made many of his future predictions as a result of hallucinations experienced through the Dream Machine technique.

Instructions

The app can be used with a Google Cardboard-style viewer, or the user can sit very close to the smartphone screen. The experience is most intense when the user lays down and is completely relaxed.
